Bringing Together Two of the Northwest's Finest Offerings:

Coffee and Salmon.

Yes, you heard right: coffee and salmon. Pat's Salmon Cookies are biscotti hand made into the shape of coho salmon, and made with locally caught fish. It's the ideal match for a steaming cup of your favorite local coffee on a gray Seattle morning.

Pat's Salmon Cookies are made from the finest local chinook and coho salmon, ground to a pulp and cooked into delightful salmon-y shapes to accompany your morning coffee-swilling.

To reduce the delicious yet sometimes overpowering slamon flavor, we slather each cookie in frosted goop!

The secret ingredient is love, which Pat gets from his long-suffering family. These kids love salmon cookies!

Feel like putting your love of local salmon on your chest, as well as in your tummy? Well now you can. Buy some salmon merchandise today!

Our region is rich in salmon-related local cheer. Check out Salmon Days in Issaquah, the Ballard Seafood Festival, and other great events!
